About Chennai Institue Of Technology

Chennai Institute of Technology stands out for its commitment to practical education and cultivating an entrepreneurial mindset among students. The institute's incubation center empowers aspiring entrepreneurs with resources to launch and grow startups. Through strategic partnerships with leading companies, students gain real-world experiences and job opportunities, setting them up for career success.

About Magnus

Magnus, the technical symposium of the department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Chennai Institute of Technology is an event that aims to celebrate the technological marvels achieved in the field of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. Join us for an exhilarating experience diving into cutting-edge technology and innovation in the field of AI and ML. Expect thought-provoking technical talks by renowned experts, interactive workshops, and hackathons that will challenge your skills and broaden your horizons.
